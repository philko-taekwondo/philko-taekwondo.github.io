Unleash Your Inner Champion

Experience the power and discipline of World Class Taekwondo. Join our community and start your journey today.

Mind & Body

More Than Combat

Our philosophy is that Taekwondo is a journey of self-improvement, developing focus, discipline, and respect. It's about building character, not just learning to fight. We foster an environment where students can grow mentally and physically.

Community

Collaborative Efforts

Join a supportive and encouraging community of all ages. We believe in lifting each other up and celebrating every achievement, from the first belt to the last. Our school is more than a dojang; it's a second home.

Expertise

Martial Excellence

Learn from acclaimed and certified masters who have dedicated their lives to the art of Taekwondo. Our instructors bring a wealth of experience, patience, and passion to every class, ensuring you receive the highest quality of training.

Your Path to Strength,

Discipline, and Confidence

At PhilKO Taekwondo, we don’t just teach martial arts—we shape champions in mind, body, and spirit. Led by Head Instructor Pablo Calatong Jr., our programs cater to all ages and skill levels, offering group classes, private training, school varsity programs, and competition coaching.

Join the PhilKO family today and experience training that builds more than just skills—it builds character, confidence, and lifelong discipline.

From our main chapter in Ayala, Olongapo to our branches in Subic and San Marcelino, we provide world-class Taekwondo instruction designed to help you achieve your goals—whether it’s fitness, self-defense, competition, or personal growth.
